Community Partners

KCDC is a floating schoolhouse that relies on community partnerships for space. Our goal is to use using existing resources to foster this learning community. By sharing homes, cafes, gardens, galleries, business spaces, and everything in between, KCDC can meet our needs for classroom space while helping the community explore the city in new and innovative ways.

We couldn’t function without the generous donation of space, time, and resources from our community partners. If you’d like to become a community partner and share your space with KCDC teachers, students, and organizers, email us at knowledgecommonsdc@gmail.com.

Many thanks to our fiscal sponsor, Day Eight, a D.C.-based arts organization.
