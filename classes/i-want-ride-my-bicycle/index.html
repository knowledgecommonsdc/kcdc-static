I Want to Ride My Bicycle!

date Sunday, 25 May 2014 time 1 p.m. to 2:30 p.m.

👤 Tiffany Joslin 🏢 Lamont Street Collective

 Past

Art by Luke Atkinson

Many people want to commute by bike to avoid long waits for Metro trains and buses, but they have no idea where to begin. This course will teach the basics to those new to the biking world or simply looking to better their bike-commuting experience.

We’ll cover:

  • Kinds of bikes and their uses
  • Where to buy a bike and new vs. used
  • Accessories
  • Bike safety
  • Bike stores around the city and what they offer

Location

Lamont Street Collective
1822 Lamont St. NW
Washington, DC 20010
Neighborhood: Mount Pleasant

Instructor

Tiffany Joslin has lived in D.C. for three years and biked here for more than two. She’s gone through two bikes with naught more than a stolen light and one minor accident (a run-in with trolley tracks). She works at a think tank and enjoys coffee, writing, rock climbing, and reading.
