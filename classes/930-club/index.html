More Than a Mic Check: A Tour of the 9:30 Club

date Thursday, 3 September 2015 time 6 p.m. to 7 p.m.

🏢 9:30 Club

 Past

This is unlikely to happen during your tour. (OK GO Drone Show, 1/21/15 by Joe Loong is licensed under CC BY-SA 2.0.)

Since 1980, the 9:30 Club has been a second home to locals seeking great live music. See the venue with the lights up — no earplugs, ID, or nightowl nature required — on this front-of-the-house tour led by 9:30 Club staff.
