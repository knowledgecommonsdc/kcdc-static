Stream Restoration in Wards 7 & 8 Bike Tour

date Saturday, 28 September 2013 time 9:30 a.m. to noon

👤 Josh Burch 🏢 Mississippi Avenue and 13th Street SE

 Past

Observe the state of the District’s streams on this tour of Wards 7 and 8. Through visits to three streams (Oxon Run, Pope Branch, and Watts Branch), you’ll come to understand the causes of environmental degradation to them; learn about the constraints and limits to restoration efforts; and see what a successful urban stream restoration project looks like.

We’ll be biking on heavily trafficked roadways and it will be a hilly ride. The class is best-suited for experienced urban riders.

Location

Mississippi Avenue and 13th Street SE
Intersection of Mississippi Ave. and 13th St. SE
Washington, DC

Past event

Instructor

Josh Burch works for the District Department of the Environment managing stream restoration projects east of the Anacostia River. Before he joined DDOE, he worked at several local non-profits, training at-risk youth for jobs in the fields of construction and environmental restoration.
