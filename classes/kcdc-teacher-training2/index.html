KCDC Teacher Training

date Thursday, 9 August 2012 time 7 p.m.

Knowledge Commons DC

 Past

Knowledge Commons works because interesting people share what they know. Our teachers range from university professors to passionate amateurs. For those of us with less teaching experience, this is an opportunity to get to know other teachers, pick up some tips on peer to peer learning, basic classroom management, and creative knowledge sharing.

There will be very brief presentations on these topics, followed by a smorgasbord of snacks, camaraderie and general merriment. Please bring an idea or two you’re interested in developing for a class and we’ll help you work on the details. Whether you are signed up to take a class, have interest in someday teaching a class, or are just curious, we look forward to learning with you.

Location

In Mount Pleasant/Petworth (house address emailed 2 days before event)


Address will be emailed upon registration.
