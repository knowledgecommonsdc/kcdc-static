Salsa for Beginners

date Sunday, 20 November 2016 time 1 p.m. to 2 p.m.

👤 Raymond Wong 🏢 Chinatown Cultural Community Center

 Past

Salsa engages the principles of natural body movement, movements designed to express ones inner energy through music. Honor this empowering art and share the joy of the dance in this introductory class.

Please wear comfortable, casual clothing. You don’t need to bring a partner or have any dancing experience whatsoever.

If you’re not able to make this date, we’ll be offering the class again on November 10.
