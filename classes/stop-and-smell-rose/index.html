Everything’s Coming Up Rosés

date Sunday, 26 June 2016 time 1 p.m. to 3 p.m.

👤 Danielle Witt 🏢 Danielle's Apartment

 Past

You’ll learn about and sample a selection of rosé wines, from a dry, pale-pink French vin to a fruity, jammy Spanish vino. We’ll cover what rosé is — and what rosé is not — while enjoying great, wallet-friendly wines (no white zinfandel here, folks!). We’ll talk about how rosé is made, how to pair the delicate flavors, and what to look for when buying a bottle.

There is a $5 materials fee for this course. All attendees must be 21 or older. Please bring cash and your I.D.

Location

Danielle's Apartment

Instructor

Danielle Witt has worked in wine retail in the D.C. area, and credits all her love and knowledge of wine to time spent at Best Cellars in Dupont Circle (RIP). She loves helping friends find the perfect bottle and demystifying snobby wine culture. A native Virginian, she understands if you don’t like Virginia wine, but can she convince you to at least try this one Cabernet Franc from Charlottesville that is really, really good? She bets you’ll like it!
