Introduction to After-Life Planning

date Thursday, 23 March 2017 time 6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

👤 Judith del Cuadro-Zimmerman 🏢 WeWork Chinatown

 Past

After-life planning can extend the impact of your life beyond the d-word. (Photo by canong2fan)

You’ve probably spent at least some time thinking about what will happen when you die. But have you actually planned for it?

Licensed attorney Judith del Cuadro-Zimmerman will share her experience in probate law (wills and related documents; see, you learned something already!) to help you think through the end-of-life documents you need to protect your assets and your family. We’ll discuss why end-of-life planning is important for folks of all ages and share tips for putting the necessary paperwork together on a budget.

Come with your questions and leave with the knowledge to put your legal house in order, regardless of your stage of life.

Location

WeWork Chinatown
718 Seventh St. NW
Washington, DC
Neighborhood: Chinatown

Instructor

Judith del Cuadro-Zimmerman practices family law, probate, and estate planning. As a co-founding partner of del Cuadro-Zimmerman & Mount, PLLC, she offers a wide range of family law services in wills, estates, and probate for the D.C. and Maryland community. When not fighting the good fight on behalf of her clients, Judith is an avid traveler (ask her about the time she drove an ambulance across Mongolia!) and yoga practitioner.
