Everything You Wanted to Know About Real Estate But Were Afraid to Ask

date Wednesday, 26 September 2012 time 7 p.m. to 9:30 p.m.

👤 Arynne Crane 🏢 DCRE

 Past

Many people are intimidated by the home purchase process and by sleazy real estate agents. This class will be a 30-45 min introduction to the basics of the home purchase process, followed by question and answer. The class will address questions such as: Is owning a home right for you? How I find a neighborhood that fits my lifestyle? What types of loans/down payment/DC home ownership programs are there? What does the settlement process (inspection, appraisal & other contingencies) entail? A lender will also be present to help answer questions about mortgages.

FYI: The teacher of this course is not trying to get money from her students! There is no cost to use a realtor to purchase a home; the commissions are paid by sellers, not buyers. Therefore, the instructors is not trying to teach this course and get money from students. Rather, she thinks that creative people should be educated on the home ownership and investment process and wants to share her knowledge with people that that beautify their neighborhoods and improve their communities.

Instructor

It’s pronounced Erin, but spelled Arynne. But Arynne’s unique name is just the beginning…

Perhaps the writing was on the wall when Arynne developed her own cookie recipe at just five years of age. A woman who never lacks for creativity, Arynne has always done things her own individual way. Take her name, for instance. Pronounced “Erin,” her mother put a unique twist on the spelling, and in the process, set a course for Arynne’s individuality to flourish.

Carving her own path Arynne still enjoys cooking, and not surprisingly, she relishes tinkering with flavors to add a little unexpected kick to any recipe she develops. Anyone who’s tried her cinnamon and cayenne-infused brownies can attest to the success of her creativity. The same goes for her key lime pie, which includes a splash of tequila.

Arynne’s creativity isn’t limited to the kitchen, however. She’s a “treasure hunter” by nature who enjoys antiquing and, in a previous career, was a buyer for a vintage clothing shop. She also loves painting, getting outdoors, and expanding her horizons through travel. Her unique approach also led to being named Marketing Student of the Year en route to earning her bachelor’s degree in marketing from George Mason University. And today, her ability to “think outside the box” is at the root of her success as a leading real estate professional.

A unique approach Arynne Crane’s eclectic tastes, creativity and innovation are at the root of her success in the DC real estate marketplace. Her unusual name is symbolic of the unique approach and truly unique results she achieves for her valued clients.

Unique name, truly unique results Everything about Arynne Crane is unique. From her name to her attire to her proven approach to assisting her clients, Arynne blends creativity with intuition to create “Truly Unique Results” for her clients. If you’re in the market to buy or sell a home, don’t settle for ordinary real estate service. Make the most of your next move with a truly unique real estate resource.
