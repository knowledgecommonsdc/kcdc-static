Demystify Your Finances: Mapping Your Own Financial Future

date Tuesday, 21 June 2016 time 6:30 p.m. to 8 p.m.

👤 Cady North 🏢 Tenley-Friendship Neighborhood Library

 Past

Do you find managing your personal finances to be difficult, boring, or confusing? Perhaps difficult, boring, and confusing? Does it feel impossible to decipher the multiple account types, products, tax treatments, and investments available? Don’t let the variety of choices become crippling — take action to demystify your finances!

This class will teach you how to think strategically about your priorities in order to center your personal and financial goals on those values. You’ll also create a “mind map” of your finances to quantify what you own and what you owe. Finally, you’ll master tips and tricks to help you stick to your goals.

Please bring a pen or your computer to help develop your financial future.

This class is presented in partnership with DC Public Library.

Instructor

Cady North is an entrepreneur and personal finance expert. After more than a decade in the financial services and public policy industries, she started North Financial Advisors to bring independent and affordable financial advice to individuals. Her company specializes in preparing comprehensive financial plans for women and entrepreneurs in all phases of their lives. Cady enjoys helping her clients dream big, find financial freedom, and navigate life changes.
