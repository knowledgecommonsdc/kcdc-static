Manet, Monet, and More: French Art of the Nineteenth Century

date Saturday, 9 June 2012 time 2 p.m.

Liz Tunick

 Past

Come explore some of the most popular galleries at the National Gallery of Art. Learn about 19th-century artists, trends, scandals, and, of course, art, while also enjoying a behind-the-scenes discussion of how and why the galleries are installed as they are. We’ll meet at the rotunda on the north side of the large fountain nearest Constitution Ave for a special tour of some of the most popular treasures of the gallery.

Check out some exhibit details here and come ready to learn!
