You Are the First Organization That You Must Master

date Wednesday, 5 February 2014 time 7 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

👤 Kaytura L. Felix 🏢 Cleveland Park Neighborhood Library

 Past

Leadership is the state we’re in anytime we’re faced with a decision that impacts others. It’s not just a position on an organizational chart; rather, it is a way of acting that produces results.

This course delves into following statement from “Retooling on the Run: Real Change for Leaders with No Time” by Stuart Heller and David Sheppard Surrenda:

“You are the first organization that you must master. No one can do this for you. It is your responsibility. Your results are a function of the way you organize and use yourself.”

You’ll explore the idea that every leader is a unique observer defined through language, body, and emotions. By the end of the class, you’ll have a starter kit of concepts and exercises you can use to further develop your leadership skills.

This class is uniquely suited to career changers and anyone else who’s ready for a new direction in life.

Instructor

Kaytura L. Felix, MD, is a personal and executive coach at ISL Coaching, which she founded in 2012 to help her clients enjoy meaningful success in every dimension of their lives. For more than two decades, she held leadership positions in the federal government and practiced internal medicine. Kay is a devoted mother who spends her Saturdays helping young adults develop their spiritual perceptions and powers of expression. She lives in the Washington, D.C., area with her daughter and two cats, Donald and Daisy.
