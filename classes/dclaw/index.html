D/Claw XIII Summer Smackdown – Supporting Knowledge Commons DC

♫ KCDC Event date Saturday, 28 June 2014 time 8 p.m.

👤 Knowledge Commons DC 🏢 Kenneth H. Nast Post 8

 Past

On June 28th some of DC’s toughest women, the DC Lady Arm Wrestlers, will take each other on in the ancient art form of arm wrestling. Funds raised at the bout will benefit KCDC! We’re excited about this partnership and would love to see you there.

Doors open at 8:00 pm
Match starts at 8:30 pm
Halftime: burlesque performance from Hot Night Productions

$10 admission. Bring cash to bet! Watching arm wrestling might bring about feelings of exhaustion, but you’re in luck as the Flippin’ Pizza truck will be outside. There will be a bar inside the venue.

Not sure what to expect? Read about DCLAW. Or be surprised.
