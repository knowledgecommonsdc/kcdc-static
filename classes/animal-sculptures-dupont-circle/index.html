Animal Sculptures of Dupont Circle

date Saturday, 23 February 2019 time 1 p.m. to 3 p.m.

👤 Geoff Patterson 🏢 Dupont Circle

 Past

Party Animals and PandaMania may be long gone, but some of the hundreds of donkeys, elephants, and pandas that invaded D.C. in the early 2000s remain. This walking tour will explore these and other animal sculptures in the Dupont Circle area.

We will walk about 3 miles at a leisurely pace on paved surfaces. Here’s the approximate route.

Location

Dupont Circle
Washington, DC
Meet near the fountain

Instructor
