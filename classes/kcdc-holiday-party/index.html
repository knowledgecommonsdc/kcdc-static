KCDC Holiday Party!

♫ KCDC Event date Saturday, 12 December 2015 time 5 p.m. to 7 p.m.

🏢 KCDC Pop-Up Learning Space

 Past

See how much fun we had last year?

Celebrate the holidays with KCDC! 

Join us for food, drinks (alcoholic and otherwise), music, and learning! We’ll have hors-d’oeuvres from everyone’s favorite vegan soul food restaurant, Woodland’s Vegan Bistro, plus beer, wine, sodas, a collage workshop, and a free gift swap. We will have alcohol tastings as well - sponsored by Green Hat.  We’ll be taking donations–$10 suggested–to support KCDC programs.

At 6:30 p.m. we’ll be hosting a free gift swap! If you’d like to participate, please bring a gift you’ve acquired for free. (Don’t spend any money on it!) Wrapping is recommended. We’ll exchange the gifts in a giant group swap.  

At 7 p.m., the afterparty begins at The Public Option, a lovely bar down the street, where the owners will teach us about the history of restaurant tipping and their own no-tip policy. KCDC will receive 10 percent of sales from The Public Option during the event.

The party is open to all, but please bring your ID if you would like to drink. RSVP here or via our Facebook event!
