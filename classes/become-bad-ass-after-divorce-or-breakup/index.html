Become a Bad Ass After a Divorce or Breakup

date Thursday, 27 June 2019 time 6:30 p.m. to 8 p.m.

👤 Joe Bernstein 🏢 Capitol View Library

 Past

Many of us see the loss of romantic relationships as some of our darkest times. Culturally, we are taught that a relationship not lasting the test of time is a failure. However, when you talk to people who have been through divorce or a huge relationship loss, you may start to see that their lives often improve over time.

Join professional coach Joe Bernstein for a class on the most powerful steps you can take to become a badass after a divorce, a breakup, or other personal adversity. All are welcome: Even if you are in a happy relationship, the teachings are universal in helping to create the life you love. We will cover ways that you can improve your body, your mind, your spirit, and your social and romantic life, all using adversity you face to fuel the next level of your personal growth.

This class is presented in partnership with DC Public Library.

Instructor

After losing 150 lbs, thriving after a divorce, and changing careers, Joe Bernstein is committed to helping others make change happen. He works with successful business owners, executives in tech, top individual performers, leaders, creatives, and entrepreneurs. They’ve spent their lives focusing on professional success and when they realize it’s time to get healthy and focus on family and love, Joe helps them shift the focus without sacrificing what they’ve built.
