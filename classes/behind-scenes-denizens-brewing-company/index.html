Behind the Scenes: Denizens Brewing Company

date Saturday, 17 September 2016 time 3 p.m. to 4 p.m.

👤 Emily Bruno, Jeff Ramirez, Julie Verratti 🏢 Denizens Brewing Company

 Past

Founded by Emily Bruno, Julie Verratti, and Jeff Ramirez in 2014, Denizens Brewing Company is a majority woman and minority owned and operated brewery. With diverse experiences and expertise, each founder brings something unique to the table.

During this Behind the Scenes tour, you’ll get to check out where the magic happens (hint: it’s in the brewery) and taste some of the signature brews at Denizens.

The kitchen will open if you’d like to purchase food, and please remember to tip your bartenders! This event is 21+ and has a $10 materials fee. (We waive fees when needed.)

Location

Denizens Brewing Company
1115 East-West Highway
Silver Spring, MD 20910

Past event

Emily Bruno and Julie Verratti, residents of Silver Spring, knew that the area and its denizens deserved more nightlife options. They aspired to open a venue for the community to enjoy themselves and all that craft beer has to offer, but rules had to be changed: Drawing from their political backgrounds, Emily and Julie worked with local officials to change the law to allow Denizens to self-distribute without going through the county, increasing their ability to provide fresh craft beer at reasonable prices.

Jeff Ramirez leads the brewing team at Denizens Brewing Company in its mission of making high-quality craft beer accessible to all. With years of experience in breweries from Iron Hill in New Jersey to Colorado’s Mountain Sun, Jeff brings the brewing culture of Colorado to the D.C. area. Trained at the Siebel Institute and World Brewing Academy, Jeff specializes in wild, sour, and funky beers and has won various awards for his work.
