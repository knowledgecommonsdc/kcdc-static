Discover Essential Oils

date Monday, 14 November 2016 time 7 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

👤 Priscilla Rodgers 🏢 The Third Floor/Slim's Diner

 Past

Essential oil kit by Abi Porter is licensed under CC BY 2.0.

Essential oils and aromatherapy are a staple for many seeking to empower their health and energy with the best nature has to offer. In this interactive workshop, you will learn how and when essential oils can be used in all facets of daily life, including perfumery, skin care, emotional support, feng shui, and overall health and wellness. You’ll leave armed with the knowledge of how to incorporate the oils into your life.

If you’d like to further apply your new knowledge, consider signing up for Rituals of Self Love and Care Through Essential Oils, an optional follow-up class.

Location

The Third Floor/Slim's Diner
4201 Georgia Ave. NW
Washington, DC 20011
Neighborhood: Petworth
Classes will take place on the third floor. Enter through the side door on Ninth Street NW. This location has stairs, but no elevator.

Past event

Instructor

Priscilla Rodgers is the founder of Lavender Junkie, and she teaches people how to use therapeutic essential oils for chakra healing, emotional support, spiritual alignment, self-care, and overall health and wellness. Her mission is to empower others to live a holistic, abundant life with the powerful assistance of essential oils.
