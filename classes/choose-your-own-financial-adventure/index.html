Choose Your Own Adventure: Radical Financial Planning

date Thursday, 6 April 2017 time 6:30 p.m. to 8 p.m.

👤 Kate and Alex Burch 🏢 The Third Floor/Slim's Diner

 Past

Which path will you take? We’ll help you choose based on your values. (Photo by www.GlynLowe.com.)

Don’t like the idea of working all the livelong day? Interested in extreme philanthropy, traveling the world, or self-funding your own business or nonprofit?

Our discussion-based class will teach you how to make the seemingly impossible become financially plausible. We’ll identify and discuss your values and lifestyle goals, learn how they should impact your finances, and chart a new financial plan for your life.

No sophisticated financial knowledge is required, just an open mind.

Location

The Third Floor/Slim's Diner
4201 Georgia Ave. NW
Washington, DC 20011
Neighborhood: Petworth
Classes will take place on the third floor. Enter through the side door on Ninth Street NW. This location has stairs, but no elevator.

Past event

Instructor

Kate and Alex Burch are young professionals in the midst of choosing their own adventure by moving to the Midwest and opening a small business. They lived in D.C. for nearly 3 years, working as a professional researcher and cost accountant (respectively). During that time, they got unnecessarily excited about the intersection of personal finance, morality, and radicalism. They blog on the practical application of these topics at Spend Your Values.
