After These Messages: Dissecting Ads on TV

date Thursday, 24 September 2015 time 8 p.m. to 10:30 p.m.

👤 playbackthetape 🏢 Petworth Citizen & Reading Room

 Past

From its very inception, television has presented an additional vehicle for advertisers to hawk their wares. Sometimes subliminal, but more often overt, TV entertainment is constantly interrupted/sponsored/defined by “messages” intended to $ell, $ell, $ell.

This show emphasizes audience discussion, while utilizing content from the 1980s/1990s to explore how advertising conventions (air times, scripts, gender stereotypes) and exhibition conventions (editing, “brought to you by…,” promotional “specials”) have evolved.

To RSVP, visit the Facebook event page or Eventbrite. 

This is not a KCDC event. It is produced by another organization. We include it because we think that it will be of interest to our students.
