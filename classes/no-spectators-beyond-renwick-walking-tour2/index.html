No Spectators: Beyond the Renwick Walking Tour

date Saturday, 26 May 2018 time 1 p.m. to 3:30 p.m.

👤 Jessica Unger 🏢 Renwick Gallery

 Past

Make sure you also check out the exhibition inside the Renwick! (Photo by Victoria Pickering.)

As we explore the outdoor installations that accompany the “No Spectators: The Art of Burning Man” exhibition at the Renwick Gallery, you’ll learn about how these objects were conceived, built, and displayed. How have the meanings of these installations changed in their different settings?

On this walking tour, we’ll examine these temporary public artworks and discuss themes in D.C.’s public art more broadly.

Location

Renwick Gallery
1661 Pennsylvania Ave NW
Washington, DC 20006
Meet outside the front doors of the building on the street so we can start our walking tour!

Past event

Instructor

Jess Unger is a public historian who spends her days at the Foundation of the American Institute for Conservation, helping museums, archives, and libraries connect with emergency planning resources.
