Creating Sustainable Dreams: A Model for Transformation

date Monday, 23 February 2015 time 7 p.m. to 8 p.m.

👤 Catherine Wood, Robin Silverman 🏢 St. Thomas' Episcopal Church

 Past

Have you ever stopped to consider whether the choices you make each and every day align with your core values? How would your life shift if the career choices you made, the relationships and friendships you cultivated, and the ways in which you fill your time matched these core values?

In this one-hour workshop, life coaches Robin and Catherine will guide you in connecting with those core values in a profound way. You will walk away from the workshop with a model for transforming your choices into value-based dreams and the knowledge to lead your life in a more gratifying and purposeful way.

Location

St. Thomas' Episcopal Church
1772 Church St. NW
Washington, DC
Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Past event

Instructors

Catherine Wood is the founder and head coach of the personal coaching firm Unbounded Potential LLC. She helps people fall in love with themselves first so they can take bolder actions, push past their fears, and get bigger results in the direction of their dreams. Her adventures — growing up in a family-run bed and breakfast, serving as a Peace Corps volunteer in the Dominican Republic, and managing ecotourism startups for two years — have shown her what dreamers can achieve through thinking big.

email catherine@catherineawood.com

🌎 catherineawood.com

Robin Silverman is a transformational and corporate coach. She inherently respects the mystery, brilliance, and wonder of who you are. Robin and her clients uncover, empower and create the lives of their dreams. She is your fellow traveler, shining light on your light.
