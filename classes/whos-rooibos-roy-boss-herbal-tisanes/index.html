Who’s the Rooibos (ROY-boss)? Herbal Tea Basics

date Monday, 9 February 2015 time 7 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

👤 Erika Rydberg 🏢 Erika's Apartment

 Past

Learn the basics of herbal teas (aka tisanes), and kick off 2015 caffeine-free!

We’ll discuss:

  • What makes herbal teas herbal;
  • Tisane terminology;
  • The health benefits of various tisanes.

Then we’ll taste-test some common varieties, including the eponymous rooibos.

By the end of the class, you’ll be ready to navigate your local tea shop and/or grocery aisle with ease. And you’ll know how “tisane” is pronounced. (It’s not “tih-sane.”)

Please bring a mug and $5 for the tasting.
