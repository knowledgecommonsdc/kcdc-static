Class Ideas and Requests

Can you teach these classes? We’ve had requests for classes on these topics.

For any session

  • History of musical theater
  • Christmas carols in foreign languages
  • Ballet
  • City planning and walkability/multimodal transportation
  • Falconry and raptors
  • Esperanto or constructed languages
  • Animal training (especially homing pigeons)
  • Horseback riding
  • The war on drugs
  • Government surveillance
  • Minority languages (like Kurdish, Catalan, Gaelic, Maori, Romansch)
  • Sleight of hand and misdirection
  • Human rights in Russia
  • Astronomy
  • Ornithology and bird watching
  • Strategies for playing Risk or Monopoly
  • Dia de Muertos traditions
  • Early feminism history
  • OpenStreetMap, either a practical class or a critical one
  • African languages, Xhosa and Zulu in particular
  • How to whistle
  • Traditional folk dances from around the world
  • Greek Mythology (or Norse, Roman, Chinese, Egyptian, Korean, etc.)
  • Knife skills for cooking
  • How finance works (not how to invest, but more about what goes on behind the scenes)
  • Synchronized swimming
  • Syrian cooking
  • Shorthand
