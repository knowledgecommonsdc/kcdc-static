Press Kit

Hi there!

Welcome to the Knowledge Commons DC press kit.

We’ve compiled some basic facts you might need in order to write about us. Stats included!

We’ve also chosen some nice hi-res photos for you, with credits and everything, and have KCDC logos in a range of formats. 

Please email contact@knowledgecommonsdc.org with questions, interview requests, and so forth. If you need to talk to someone ASAP, you may call Erika at 508-813-0019.

If you’d like to attend a class — either to write about it or to generally get acquainted with our organization — email contact@knowledgecommonsdc.org at least 24 hours in advance with the name of the class you’d like to take, your contact info, and, if you’re not on staff at a publication, something that shows you’re a journalist. In nearly all cases we’ll be able to accommodate you.
